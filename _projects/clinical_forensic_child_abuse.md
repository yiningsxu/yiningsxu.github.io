## Clinical Forensic Assessment of Child Abuse

**Project:** Retrospective study on abuse assessment in clinical forensic medicine

- **Period:** December 2025 - March 2030
- **Institutions:** C University (lead), Kyoto University
- **Role:** Co-investigator: data analysis

This project evaluates clinical forensic medicine pathways for suspected child abuse cases. The analysis focuses on clinical findings, background risks, assessment processes, and decision-making to support evidence-based implementation.
