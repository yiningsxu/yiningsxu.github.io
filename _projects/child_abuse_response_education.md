## Child Abuse Response and Educational Intervention

**Project:** Current status of child abuse response and the impact of educational interventions among firefighters, hospital paramedics, and paramedic students

- **Period:** January 2026 - March 2028
- **Institutions:** T University (lead), Kyoto University
- **Role:** Co-investigator: data analysis

This collaborative study examines the current state of child abuse response among emergency and prehospital care professionals and students. It also evaluates the impact of educational interventions to strengthen recognition and response capacity.
