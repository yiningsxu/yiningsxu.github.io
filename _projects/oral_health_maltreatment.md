## Oral Health and Maltreatment Classification

**Project:** Analysis of the relationship between oral health characteristics and maltreatment classifications among children in temporary protection

- **Period:** September 2024 - March 2027
- **Institutions:** Kyoto University (lead), A Prefecture Dental Association
- **Role:** Principal investigator: planning, operation, analysis, and manuscript writing

This study uses dental checkup and child welfare data to quantify how oral health conditions relate to maltreatment classifications. The project aims to produce evidence that can support early risk assessment and practical collaboration between dental and child welfare professionals.
