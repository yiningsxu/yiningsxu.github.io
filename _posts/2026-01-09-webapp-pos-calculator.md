### I built a “[Smart POS Calculator](https://yiningsxu.github.io/pos_calculator/)” (mobile-first web app)
I made a lightweight, mobile-first web app that turns a smartphone into a simple POS: a calculator plus customizable product buttons.<br>
You can open it instantly in the browser (no install needed), and once it’s loaded it can also work offline.

I made this app for my friend who runs a shop.<br>
<span style="color:red">If you want to add any features, please let me know via email!</span> <br>

### What it does
- **Custom product buttons (0–5)** with editable **prices and colors** for quick sales
- **Smart counting** (e.g., tap the same button multiple times to increase quantity)
- **Quick multiplier** button (**×1.1**) for tax/service charge
- **Cash/change flow** with a “Cash / Received” step
- **Sales history stored locally** in the browser
- **CSV export** for Excel/Google Sheets
- **Multi-language UI** (English / Japanese / Chinese)

### Why I made it
For small shops, stalls, and event booths, speed matters more than complexity. I wanted something you can open immediately, operate with one hand, and still leave you with usable sales records (CSV) afterward.
